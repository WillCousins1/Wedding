How to Bloom Your Invitation 🌸
Your invitation is printed on seeded paper — plant it and watch wildflowers grow!
1️⃣
Tear or cut into pieces
Break your seeded paper into small pieces roughly 2–3cm in size. Smaller pieces help the seeds germinate more easily.
2️⃣
Choose your pot or patch
Use a small pot with compost or a sunny spot in the garden. Make sure the soil is loose, moist and well-drained.
3️⃣
Place the paper on the soil
Lay the pieces flat on top of the soil and gently press them down so they're in good contact with the compost.
4️⃣
Cover lightly with soil
Sprinkle a thin layer of compost (about 3mm) over the top of the paper pieces to help them bed in.
5️⃣
Water gently & keep moist
Water lightly using a fine spray or misting bottle. Keep the soil moist but not waterlogged. Place in a warm, bright spot — a sunny windowsill is perfect.
🌱
Watch them grow!
Seedlings should start to appear within 1–2 weeks. Once established, move outdoors and enjoy your blooms — a little reminder of our special day. 🌸
💡 Best time to plant: Spring and autumn are ideal. Avoid planting during frost or in very hot, dry conditions. The wildflowers love British weather!
